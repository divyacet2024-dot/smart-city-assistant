# Smart Bin - Smart City Waste Management System

## Project Overview

**Smart Bin** is an innovative web-based application that leverages IoT technology and Artificial Intelligence to revolutionize waste management in smart cities. The system connects citizens, garbage collection authorities, and waste management facilities through a unified digital platform, making recycling easier and more efficient.

---

## Problem Statement

Urban waste management faces critical challenges:
- **Inefficient Collection Routes**: Garbage trucks follow fixed schedules regardless of actual bin fill levels
- **Low Recycling Rates**: Citizens lack knowledge about proper waste sorting andOverflowing recycling
- ** Bins**: Unpredictable waste accumulation leads to unhygienic conditions
- **Lack of Citizen Engagement**: No gamification or incentives for proper waste disposal
- **Poor Communication**: No real-time feedback between citizens and authorities

---

## Proposed Solution

Smart Bin creates a connected ecosystem where:
- **IoT-enabled bins** report fill levels in real-time
- **AI-powered waste identification** helps citizens sort recyclables correctly
- **Smart routing algorithms** optimize garbage collection paths
- **Gamification features** encourage citizens to participate in sustainable waste management
- **Authority dashboard** provides city administrators with actionable insights

---

## Key Features

### 1. AI Waste Scanner
- Upload images of waste items
- AI automatically identifies waste type (plastic, paper, glass, metal, organic, e-waste)
- Provides instant recycling tips and proper disposal guidance

### 2. AI Recycling Assistant
- Chatbot powered by Google Gemini AI
- Answers questions about recycling, composting, and waste management
- Provides localized guidelines based on user's area

### 3. Smart Bin Dashboard (IoT Simulation)
- Real-time visualization of bin fill levels
- Color-coded status indicators (green/yellow/red)
- Historical fill-level trends
- Predictive analytics for waste accumulation

### 4. Smart Routing System
- Interactive map showing bin locations
- Optimized collection route planning
- Distance and time estimations
- Priority-based collection for full bins

### 5. Gamification & Leaderboard
- Eco-points system for proper waste disposal
- Weekly/Monthly leaderboards
- Achievement badges and rewards
- Community challenges

### 6. Authority Portal
- Admin dashboard for city waste management officials
- Data analytics and reports
- Bin management system
- Collection schedule management
